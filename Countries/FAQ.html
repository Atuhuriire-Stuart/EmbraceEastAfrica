Passport Questions

The standard East African Passport is valid for 10 years for adults and 5 years for minors (under 18 years).

Yes, the East African Passport is recognized internationally, though visa requirements depend on destination countries. It has the same validity as national passports for international travel.

The East African Passport facilitates easier movement within the EAC region while maintaining all functions of your national passport. It serves as both a national and regional travel document.

Travel Questions

No, East African citizens can travel freely within the EAC region without visas. However, you must carry valid identification (national ID or passport).

Yes, most EAC countries allow crossing with national IDs, but we recommend carrying your passport for international-standard identification.

Business Questions

Yes, the EAC Common Market Protocol allows citizens to establish businesses in any partner state with the same rights as nationals of that country.

Yes, small-scale cross-border traders can obtain an EAC Simplified Certificate of Origin which grants preferential treatment at borders.
