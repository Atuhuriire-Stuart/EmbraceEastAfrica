About Swahili Language

Swahili (Kiswahili) is a Bantu language spoken by over 100 million people across East Africa. It serves as:

  • The official language of Tanzania and Kenya
  • The lingua franca of the East African Community
  • One of the working languages of the African Union
  • A language taught in universities worldwide

Learning Swahili will enhance your experience when visiting any East African country and help you connect with local communities.
